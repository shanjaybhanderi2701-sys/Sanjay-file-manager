Filora — InShot-feel Hi-Fi

APP-134 craft pass, realigned to the total feel of InShot / XFolder and the ratified blue design system (the earlier Iris/purple pass is retired). Grounded in the Chief's benchmark study of the reference app's 8 screenshots — the premium cues it isolated: colorful custom per-type icons, storage as the hero (volume cards + % rings), content-type color-coded rows with mini content badges, source-grouped media, and Cleaner/utility value surfaces — while keeping Filora calmer, ad-free, and accessible.
