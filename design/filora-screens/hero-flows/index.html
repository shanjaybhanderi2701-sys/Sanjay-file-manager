Filora — Hero Flows

The two hero flows the Taste Guide asked for, in the Filora Iris design language. A — Browse (content-first, all five states) and B — Multi-select bottom action bar (the InShot instinct: the object is the interface, big single-purpose tools in the thumb zone). Every value here comes from the token system in 07-design-system.md — taste, not theft.
