Filora — Hero Flows · BLUE variant (for review)

The two flows people actually spend time in — browsing files and batch-acting on a selection — rendered in a BLUE primary variant for board review (vs the ratified Iris/purple) with a 3-tab InShot-style bar — Home · Recent · Cleaner and filled tab icons (Browse nests under Home). Applies the Play Store Taste Guide (APP-136): content-first chrome, warmth in the empty states, honest & reversible destructive actions, and an InShot-style bottom action bar where the object is the interface. One accent (blue) plus the per-category accent wheel — no second brand color.
