## 1. Listing text (ready to paste)

**App name:** Filora — File Manager *(≤ 30 chars; "Filora" is a working brand,
confirm final per PRD open question)*

**Short description** *(≤ 80 chars):*
> Fast, private, ad-free file manager. Browse, search & organize — fully offline.

**Full description** *(≤ 4000 chars; original copy):*
> Filora is a fast, private, fully native file manager for Android. Browse,
> search, and organize everything on your phone and on SD/USB/cloud-document
> storage — with no ads, no sign-up, and nothing sent off your device.
>
> **Browse anything**
> • Internal, SD card, USB, and cloud-document storage by folder
> • Smooth even in folders with 10,000+ items
> • Light & dark themes, phone & tablet layouts
>
> **Find it fast**
> • Search by name, type, size, and date across whole trees
> • Category hubs: Images, Video, Audio, Documents, Downloads, APKs, Archives
>
> **Get things done**
> • Copy, move, rename, delete, and create folders with smart conflict handling
> • Multi-select for batch operations, with progress that survives backgrounding
> • Safe by design: move verifies before deleting the source; destructive
>   actions confirm first
> • Compress to ZIP and extract archives
> • Open and share any file with the right app via the system sheet
>
> **Privacy first**
> • No ads, no accounts, no trackers
> • Works fully offline — your files never leave your device
> • Minimal, scoped permissions requested only when needed
>
> Filora is original software by appblish.
